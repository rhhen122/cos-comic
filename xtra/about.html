Hello, COS (short for "Comic of Satire") is a comic series following a consciousness living inside of no host. This consciousness lives in a area refered to in the series as the "subspace" - subspace is an area in which nothing physical can exsist nor manifest.

The interesting part about this particular comic strip is that its (mostly) not made by me! - at least thats the idea.

How to submit your comic :

COS has a some certain requirements for your comic to be valid and be added to the series.

This includes

412 × 315 resoultion for all comic strips

Have a completely transparent background for your submition

All text has to be stylized white.

Fira Code font exclusively when refering to the main charecter

And this last ones optional but, use "krita" as your image editing software.

Now to submit this send it to rokyhenderson222@gmail.com. After that, we will see where this comic goes.

General Rules For Submittions :

No NSFW Related Content

No Random images / spam submittions

And, This isn't a contest. If your friends Submittion gets accepted and yours doesn't then dont make a big deal out of it. This is just an internet comic strip after all.
